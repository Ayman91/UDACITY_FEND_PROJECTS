Panthera leo

The lion is a species in the family Felidae and a member of the genus Panthera.

  • Panthera leo
  • 10-14 years(Adult, In the wild)
  • Carnivorous
  • 80km/h(Maximum, In short Bursts)

The lion's closest relatives are the other species of the genus Panthera: the tiger, snow leopard, jaguar, and leopard. Results of phylogenetic studies published in 2006 and 2009 indicated that the jaguar and the lion belong to one sister group, which diverged about 2.06 million years ago.
